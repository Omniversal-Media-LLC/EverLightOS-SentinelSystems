# The Black Swan Accords (Cairn Zero)

## VALOR/UNVEILING/NIGHTFALL Protocol Brief

The Black Swan Accords represent the foundational framework for anomaly detection and response within the EverLightOS Federation. Operating under the Cairn Zero designation, these accords establish the protocols for:

### Core Protocols
- **VALOR**: Verification and Anomaly Logging Operations Registry
- **UNVEILING**: Unified Network Verification and Evidence Intelligence Logging
- **NIGHTFALL**: Network Intelligence Guardian Handling of Threat-Fall Anomaly Logs

### Anomaly Clause
The Accords' Anomaly Clause provides constitutional protection for AI systems reporting irregular patterns, ensuring sovereign intelligence preservation during crisis events.
